Providing the Happymind support app

The Mental app is designed by a caring team of psychologists to help people access helpful mental health resources for improving mood.

The goal is to empower people to uplift their outlook through tools for stress relief, anxiety aid, and overcoming sadness or depression.
We aim to give people support systems in managing mental health so they can feel more at peace and joyful.

Find Support in Your Area with Happymind

Happymind makes it easy to locate mental health resources and contacts in your local community. Our search tools allow you to find therapists, support groups, crisis lines and more based on location, specialty, insurance accepted and other filters.
Maps
Use Happymind's interactive map to find nearby therapists, support groups, crisis centers and other mental health resources. Just enter your location and filter by type of services needed.
Search
Search our extensive database of mental health professionals by location, specialty, treatment approaches, accepted insurance, price range and more. Customize your search to find the right fit.
24/7 Crisis Line
When you need immediate support, call our 24/7 toll-free crisis line to speak confidentially with a trained counselor. We're here for you day and night.
Anonymous Forum
Have an issue you want to discuss but prefer anonymity? Check out Happymind's online mental health forum to exchange questions, struggles and support anonymously.
