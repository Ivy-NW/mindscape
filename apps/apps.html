Mental health app categories

We offer five main categories of mental health mobile apps to help understand emotions.

Mood trackers

Track and analyze moods over time to reveal patterns and triggers. Featured apps include Daylio, eMoods, Moodpath.

Journaling

Write about thoughts and feelings privately to process emotions. Apps like Journey, Penzu, Carbon Paper provide guided journal templates.

Meditation

Practice mindfulness meditation through apps like Calm, Headspace, Insight Timer to relieve stress.
